# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 5, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the C2 Avanzare Photo Organizer Bot ("App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Telegram bot service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Dropbox Access
- We collect and store a **refresh token** from your Dropbox account to upload photos
- We do NOT store your Dropbox password
- We do NOT access your Dropbox files beyond uploading to designated folders
- We do NOT copy or view your files

### 2. Telegram Information
- We receive your Telegram user ID and chat ID to communicate with you
- We store temporary file paths during the upload process (deleted after upload)
- We keep a local history of your recent uploads (last 10 uploads)

### 3. Photos and Files
- Photos you send are temporarily downloaded to our server
- After uploading to your Dropbox, temporary files are immediately deleted
- We do NOT store copies of your photos
- We do NOT share your photos with anyone

## What We Do With Your Information

- **Upload photos** to your Dropbox in the folders you select
- **Send messages** via Telegram to guide you through the process
- **Store upload history** locally on our server (only recent 10 uploads, per user)
- **Refresh your Dropbox token** automatically when needed

## What We DO NOT Do

- ❌ We do NOT sell your data
- ❌ We do NOT share your information with third parties
- ❌ We do NOT access files outside the designated upload folder
- ❌ We do NOT store your photos permanently
- ❌ We do NOT use your data for analytics or advertising

## Security

- Refresh tokens are stored securely in environment variables
- Temporary files are deleted immediately after upload
- All communication with Dropbox uses encrypted HTTPS
- The bot runs on a secure cloud server with limited access

## Dropbox Integration

This app uses the Dropbox API to upload files to your Dropbox account. Your use of Dropbox is also subject to Dropbox's Privacy Policy: https://www.dropbox.com/privacy

## Your Rights

You can:
- Stop using the bot at any time
- Revoke the app's access to your Dropbox in your Dropbox settings
- Request deletion of your upload history by contacting us
- Delete the refresh token from Dropbox console anytime
